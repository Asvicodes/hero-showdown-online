2 Device Browser Game

Hero Showdown Online

Create a room on one device, join it from another, and battle with private hero cards using your built-in offline deck.

How it works

One device starts the local Python server. Both devices then open the same app URL, join the same room code, and play turn by turn.

Each player only sees their own current card. The opponent card stays hidden until a round finishes.
